Contacting Us

Issues

To report issues, request new features or ask questions please use the Issues tab in the LazyStack github repository.

We track issues for both the LazyStack libraries and LazyStack generator under the LazyStack project.

Insight Sciences Corporation

LazyStack is sponsored by Insight Sciences Corporation (InSciCo). Our team are the original authors and maintainers of LazyStack.

Contact us at LazyStack@inscico.com to discuss direct support and bespoke development.
