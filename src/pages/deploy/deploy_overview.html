Deployment

At some point you will want to deploy your stack to production. In some cases the stack might be deployed by others. We suggest you author a Deployment Guide for your stack. We provide a Deployment Guide Checklist outline to get you started. In this section we point you to AWS documentation that is relevant to building and deploying AWS stacks. In addition, we provide a Deployment Guide example for the tutorial's PetStore stack.
